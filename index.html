Reason for Adapting AI

AI is used in automobiles now and it now only helps them navigate, but a study shows that AI-powered cars save more fuel than normal automobiles.
AI can therefore greatly increase the efficiency of water management and conservation in dams, reservoirs, industries etc. In this time where water conservation is the demand, AI is a must-use...

Barriers to AI Adaptation

There are many limitations in the capabilities of Ai in India. There is also lack of skilled programmers who can build an efficient AI Algorithm.

There is also a shortage of machines which are connected to IoT to be able to work with AI. Specially in India people do not have trust on AI in taking over control of big Machines. Moreover, In the present times of India's poverty, the government avoids investing in such big programmes.

Capabilities of AI in managing Big Projects

AI's biggest power is that it learns by mistakes and experiences, just like us. As it consists of neural networks driven by machine learning, It just keeps getting better & better.

It analyses more and deeper data using multilayered neural networks with incredible accuracy.
Self-learning algorithms make it human-like and thus,reliable.

This is why AI is capable of managing big projects.

Limitations of AI

The biggest limitation of AI is the lack of availability of data. AI is self-learning -The more the data is fed, the greater the accuracy of AI would be.AI is made to analyse and learn by data.So,more data is the key.

Shortage of skills is another problem.The lack of avaliability of technical staff with the experience of developing and operating AI solutions.

Cost is also a major factor.Deploying AI needs humongous amount of electricity and energy.It requires the best of all hardware and software which is very costly.
